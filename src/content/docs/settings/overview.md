## Accessing settings

Go to **Settings** from the dashboard. The settings page is organized into sections:

- **Profile** — your name and account details.
- **Notifications** — how and when you receive alerts.
- **Appearance** — theme and display preferences.
- **Security** — account access controls.
- **Billing** — your plan and payment information.

## Changing the theme

The fastest way to switch between light and dark mode is the **sun/moon icon** in the top bar. This is persisted per browser.

## What's New

Click **What's New** at the bottom of the sidebar to read the latest release notes and see what features have been added or improved in the platform.

## Signing out

Click **Sign Out** at the bottom of the sidebar.\
