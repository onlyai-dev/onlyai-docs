## Accessing chats

There are two ways to open the chats inbox:

1. **From the agency sidebar** — click **Chats** under Operations. You'll be asked to pick a model first, then you'll be taken to that model's inbox.
2. **From within a model** — click the **Chats** tab in the model sidebar. This takes you directly to that model's conversation list.

## Conversation list

The left panel shows all fan conversations for the selected model, ordered by most recent activity. Each entry shows:

- **Fan name or ID**
- **Last message preview**
- **Timestamp**

Scroll down to load more conversations (the list is infinite-scrolling).

Use the **search** field at the top of the list to filter conversations by fan name.

## Conversation thread

Click any conversation in the list to open the full message thread on the right.

The thread shows:

- **All messages** in chronological order, clearly labeled as fan messages or model responses.
- **PPV messages** inline within the thread, so you can see exactly when and what content was sent.

### Sending a message

Type in the message field at the bottom of the thread and press Enter (or click Send) to send a message manually.

:::note
Manual messages sent through the dashboard are sent from the connected OnlyFans account, just like any other message.
:::

### Translation

If a fan writes in a different language, use the **Translation language** selector (shown near the conversation header) to translate messages into your preferred language. This is for your reading convenience — it doesn't change what's sent to the fan.

## Deep-linking to a specific fan

Several parts of the dashboard link directly to a fan's conversation with a pre-selected context:

- Clicking a fan row in the **Analytics** PPV table.
- Clicking **Chat** from a content request in **AI Tasks**.
- Clicking a fan link in **Change Logs**.

These links open the Chats page for the correct model and scroll directly to that fan's conversation.

## Tips

- If a conversation shows unexpected behavior, check the **Change Logs** to see if any settings changed recently.
- To pause responses for a specific model, toggle **Active** to OFF in the model's [Controls](/models/controls/).
