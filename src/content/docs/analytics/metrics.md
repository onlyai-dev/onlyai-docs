## Agency dashboard KPIs

These KPIs appear on the main `/dashboard` page and reflect activity across all (or selected) models in your agency.

| KPI | Definition |
|-----|-----------|
| **Models** | Total number of connected and active OnlyFans accounts. |
| **Fans** | Total subscribers across all selected models. |
| **Revenue** | Total earnings (PPV + tips) in the selected date range. |
| **Messages** | Total messages sent by the AI in the selected range. |
| **PPV Sales** | Number of PPV purchases made by fans. |
| **Conversations** | Total active conversation threads in the period. |

## Analytics page metrics

### Revenue metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| **PPV Revenue** | Sum of all PPV content purchased by fans. |
| **Tips Revenue** | Sum of all direct tips received. |
| **Total Revenue** | PPV Revenue + Tips Revenue. |

### Activity metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| **Messages Sent** | Number of outgoing AI messages. |
| **Conversations Started** | New conversation threads initiated by the AI or by fans. |
| **New Subscribers** | Fans who subscribed during the selected period. |

### Efficiency metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| **PPV Conversion Rate** | `PPV Purchases ÷ PPV Offers Sent × 100%` |
| **Revenue per Message** | `Total Revenue ÷ Messages Sent` |
| **Revenue per Subscriber** | `Total Revenue ÷ Active Fans` |

## The fan funnel

The funnel tracks each fan's progression through key stages. A healthy funnel has a reasonable conversion rate at each step.

| Stage | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| New subscribers | Fans who subscribed in the period. |
| Messaged | Fans who received at least one AI message. |
| Replied | Fans who replied to the AI. |
| Offered PPV | Fans who received a PPV offer. |
| Purchased | Fans who bought PPV content. |

A large drop between "Messaged" and "Replied" suggests the welcome or opening messages need improvement. A large drop between "Offered PPV" and "Purchased" suggests the content or pricing needs review.

## Main dashboard timeseries chart

The timeseries chart on the main dashboard shows revenue and message volume over time. Use it to:

- Spot days with unusually high or low performance.
- Correlate spikes with specific actions (e.g. a new content drop, a welcome flow change).
- Track growth trends week-over-week or month-over-month.
