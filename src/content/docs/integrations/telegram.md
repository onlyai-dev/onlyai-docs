## What is the Telegram integration?

OnlyAI can send you **real-time notifications via Telegram** — for example, alerts when a model goes offline, when a significant event happens, or when your attention is needed.

## Connecting Telegram

1. Go to **Integrations** in the agency sidebar (under Account).
2. Find the **Telegram** card.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to link your Telegram account. This typically involves opening a Telegram bot link and confirming the connection.
5. Once connected, the card will show your Telegram account as linked.

## Disconnecting Telegram

If you want to stop receiving notifications:

1. Go to **Integrations**.
2. Find the Telegram card.
3. Click **Disconnect**.

:::note
Disconnecting removes the Telegram link from your account. You can reconnect at any time by following the connection steps again.
:::

## Notification types

Notifications are sent when important platform events occur. The exact notification types are configured by the platform and may include:

- Model bot stopped or went offline.
- Sales turned off automatically.
- New model added to the agency.

:::tip
Keep Telegram notifications enabled if you manage multiple models — it's the fastest way to know when something needs your attention without having to check the dashboard manually.
:::
