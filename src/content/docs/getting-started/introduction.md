OnlyAI is an AI-powered management platform for OnlyFans creators and agencies. It connects directly to your OnlyFans account and automates the most time-consuming parts of running a creator business — chatting with fans, delivering pay-per-view (PPV) content, welcoming new subscribers, and tracking revenue.

## Who is OnlyAI for?

OnlyAI is built for two types of users:

- **Creators and operators** who manage one or more OnlyFans accounts and want to automate fan interactions and content delivery.
- **Agency teams** where a team lead or manager oversees multiple models, assigns work to chatters, and monitors performance across the whole roster.

## What does OnlyAI automate?

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Chat** | Responds to fans automatically using a trained persona based on the model's profile. |
| **PPV Delivery** | Detects when a fan is ready to buy and sends the right content from your library. |
| **Welcome Flow** | Greets new subscribers with a custom message sequence and optional follow-ups. |
| **Warmup Drip** | Sends a series of messages to warm up fans who haven't been active. |

## Dashboard structure

The OnlyAI dashboard has two main contexts:

- **Agency view** — the top-level dashboard where you see all models, overall revenue, AI usage, and team settings.
- **Model view** — a focused workspace for a single model, with tabs for Chats, Content, Profile, Welcome Flow, Lists, and Settings.

You switch between models using the model switcher in the sidebar.
