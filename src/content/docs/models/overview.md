## The models directory

The **Models** page lists every OnlyFans account connected to your agency. It's the starting point for managing any individual model.

### Browsing models

You can view the list in two modes:

- **Grid view** — cards with model avatar, name, status, and quick toggles.
- **List view** — a compact table for scanning many models at once.

Your preferred view is saved automatically.

### Filtering and searching

- **Search** — type in the search field to filter by model name.
- **Status tabs** — filter by Active, Inactive, or All.
- **Agency filter** — if you have access to multiple agencies, switch between them.

### Model status at a glance

Each card or row shows whether the model is currently **active** (the AI bot is running). You can toggle the active state directly from the models list without opening the model.

## Opening a model

Click any model card or row to open that model's workspace. The sidebar will switch to model-specific navigation and show the model's **Overview** page.

## Adding a model

Click **Add Model** in the sidebar footer, or use the **Add Model** button on the Models page. This opens the OnlyFans connection wizard.

→ See [Connecting an OnlyFans Account](/models/connect-onlyfans/) for the full walkthrough.

:::note
You need the appropriate role (admin, superadmin, or team lead) to add new models to the agency.
:::
